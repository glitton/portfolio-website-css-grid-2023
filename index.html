about

After graduating with a BS in Electronics Engineering, I started my career as an audio engineer. Wanting to gain business skills, I completed an MBA from the University of San Francisco. In 2016 I dove deeper into web development building apps from scratch. By 2017, I pursued web development as well as devops.

I do freelance web development as well as pre and post-sales engineering for various start-up companies. Organizations benefit from my technical skills along with my business acumen to enable customers to get the most out of the solutions that I represent whether it be building a professional website and optimizing front-end performance to understanding and mitigating issues before it impacts the business.
